The Importance of Financial Literacy

Financial Literacy is an integral part of American society, money dictates many aspects of our lives. However for many people they want to dicatate their own lives, noone really wants to live paycheck to paycheck. According to U.S. Census Bureau's 2015 survey, 43.1 million Americans live in poverty. 1 in 3 Americans are either below the poverty line or just above. The statistics sited are to help make the case for the importance of financial literacy and planning. This website is intended to be a one stop shop for information regarding
finance and education. The information is presented in such a way that anyone who visits the website can learn to understand budgeting and finances at its most basic levels. The website contains educational video's on various topics from money to retirment plans to the stock market for begginers. If you become a member news letters can be provided to members. Access to financial planning tools and calculators that will can be used to help people make a variety of big financial decisions.
This website is intended for begginers and will give visitor's the confidence they need to buy a vehicle, a home, start their own business or just break away from simply surviving. Financial independence is attainable for anybody with the desire to learn and grow as a person. Thank you for visiting this site and we hope to be the facilitators on your new financial journey.
